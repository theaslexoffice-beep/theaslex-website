About the Firm

Built on depth, not breadth.

The AS Lex operates at the intersection of tax law, securities regulation, and financial crime defence. We work in a space where numbers, statutes, and strategy converge — advising clients on both the assessee side and as counsel to the revenue.


Our practice spans statutory tribunals and quasi-judicial forums across India, with a strong emphasis on representation, written submissions, and the kind of drafting that actually moves the needle. We believe in depth over breadth — fewer matters, better outcomes.

What We Do

Practice Areas

⚖️

Income Tax

Assessments, reassessments, search & seizure matters, and appeals before the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT). Both assessee-side defence and revenue-side representation. From Section 148 notices to penalty proceedings.

📊

Securities Law

SEBI investigations, insider trading (PIT Regulations), takeover code compliance, LODR obligations, and regulatory advisory. Representation before SEBI and SAT for listed companies, intermediaries, and market participants.

🏛️

Companies Act & NCLT

Corporate insolvency under IBC, oppression and mismanagement, winding-up, scheme of arrangement, and company law advisory. Representation before NCLT, NCLAT, and the SFIO.

🔍

PMLA & White Collar

Prevention of Money Laundering Act proceedings, ED investigations, provisional attachment challenges, prosecution complaints, and white-collar defence — from dawn raids to adjudication.

📋

GST & Indirect Tax

GST assessments, audits, demand and recovery proceedings, classification disputes, input tax credit issues, and advisory on indirect tax structuring and compliance.

✍️

Drafting & Advisory

Written submissions, legal opinions, representations, transactional advisory, and research memos where exceptional drafting is the difference. Quality is non-negotiable.
