About The Practice

A specialised practice at the intersection of tax, securities, and corporate law.

The AS Lex represents clients across India in matters of income tax, GST, securities regulation, corporate governance, and economic offences. We appear before tribunals, regulatory authorities, and superior courts, advocating for both revenue and private interests.

Our approach combines technical precision with strategic advocacy, drawing on expertise in accounting, finance, and law to deliver results that matter. We handle complex litigation involving reassessment proceedings, penalty disputes, SEBI enforcement, NCLT matters, and PMLA cases.

With experience on both sides of the aisle - revenue-side work and assessee representation - the firm brings a comprehensive perspective to every engagement.
